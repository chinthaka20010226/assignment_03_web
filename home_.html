About Lyceum

Founded in 1993 by visionary educator Honourable Dr. Mohan Lal Grero, the co-educational school started with only seven students and four teachers. Dr. Grero's aim was to provide comprehensive English medium education from pre-primary grades till Grade 12. Today, the Lyceum Group of Schools are under the care of Dr. Kumari Grero who functions as the Coordinating Principal.

At Lyceum, we are committed to the pursuit of excellence in teaching as well as in learning. We follow stringent quality standards when it comes to our academic and co-curricular programmes. Despite being an International School, Lyceum students grow in a multi-ethnic, multi-cultural and multi-religious school environment allowing them to be global citizens with strong roots.
